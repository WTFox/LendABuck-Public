About

LendABuck makes it easier for us to help each other in times of need. We believe that everyone needs a little help now and again and most people that need a little help will repay the one that helped them and not mind to pay a small price for assistance. LendABuck is built around that concept.

LendABuck Enables individuals to post up an amount of money they need($100 Max) and how long they need it(14 days max) and allows others to submit offers to the Borrower that then can be accepted or rejected.
